Adobo

Category: Main Course
Serves: 4 people
Price: ₱350

Adobo's enduring popularity stems from its simplicity and versatility. The basic recipe, with its soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic base, can be adapted to include various meats, vegetables, and spices.

Taste the essence of Filipino cuisine with our Adobo! Tender meat simmered in a rich, savory sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. A timeless classic that never disappoints

Sinigang

Category: Soup
Serves: 4-5 people
Price: ₱400

Sinigang's defining characteristic is its tangy broth, which awakens the palate with its refreshing sourness. Tamarind is the most common souring agent, but other fruits like calamansi, guava, or kamias can also be used.

Warm your soul with our Sinigang! A flavorful, tangy broth simmered with fresh vegetables and your choice of meat. A comforting Filipino soup that's both hearty and refreshing

Liempo

Category: Grilled
Serves: 3-4 people
Price: ₱450

Liempo is a celebration of pork belly's rich, fatty flavor. The crispy skin, achieved through grilling or deep-frying, provides a delightful contrast to the tender, juicy meat. The marinade, typically featuring garlic, soy sauce, and vinegar, infuses the pork with savory and aromatic flavors.

Savor the rich, savory flavors of our Liempo. Marinated in a special blend of spices and grilled to perfection, this pork belly dish is a true taste of Filipino comfort food.

Halo-Halo

Category: Dessert
Serves: 2 people
Price: ₱180

Halo-Halo is a Filipino dessert that transcends mere refreshment; it's a celebration. Picture layers of silky sweetened beans, chewy nata de coco, and delicate strands of gulaman, all mingling with the coolness of shaved ice and the richness of evaporated milk.

Enjoy the mountain of sweet goodness! Our Halo-Halo features, sweetened beans, nata de coco, gulaman, jackfruit, topped with leche flan, ube ice cream, and a sprinkle of toasted pinipig rice.
